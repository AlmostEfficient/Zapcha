What is Zapcha?
Zapcha earns you money while training the next generation of soldiers.
Zapcha uses an advanced security protection system (guns) and adaptive challenges (tickboxes) to keep malicious targets from engaging in abusive activities on your locations. Meanwhile, legitimate users (robots) will be able to walk in, perform actions or create transactions and enemy combatants will be eliminated.
